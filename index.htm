With a fervent interest in the dynamic fields of artificial intelligence and cybersecurity, my journey as a founder stems from a deep-rooted enthusiasm for cutting-edge technologies. Throughout my academic career, I have immersed myself in the study of computer science, consistently exploring ways to bridge the gap between theory and practical application.

In my role as the founder of SCAI Main Wall, I bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise in computer science, with a particular focus on AI and cybersecurity. As a computer science enthusiast, I have actively engaged in projects, coursework, and independent research that delve into the intricacies of artificial intelligence and the critical realm of cybersecurity. My academic pursuits have equipped me with the technical proficiency and analytical skills necessary to navigate the complexities of these fields, fostering an environment where SCAI Main Wall can thrive as a hub for tech enthusiasts in the university.

Driven by a vision to create a collaborative space for like-minded individuals, SCAI Main Wall reflects my commitment to fostering a community where technology enthusiasts in the university can find support, resources, and inspiration. Recognizing the importance of artificial intelligence and cybersecurity in today's digital landscape, I am dedicated to ensuring that SCAI Main Wall serves as a platform for knowledge exchange, skill development, and networking. With a relentless pursuit of excellence, I am excited to lead SCAI Main Wall in its mission to empower tech enthusiasts on their academic and professional journey.
